Overview

The GINICameraController controls the camera devices, frame processing and then the image capturing. It informs the rest of the app of the results via a delegate that is described in the CameraControllerDelegate protocol..

frameProcessing

Whether or not the frame processing is enabled. Frame processing means that the GINICameraController processes the camera’s preview image and tries to find a document in it. When the frame processing is enabled, the didProcessFrame delegate method is called every time the result of a frame processing is available.
